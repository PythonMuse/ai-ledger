# PythonMuse Brand Color Guide

Color palette extracted from the PythonMuse logo. Use this guide to maintain consistent branding across all documents and presentations.

---

## Core Palette

| Role | Color Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|-----------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Primary** | Deep Navy | `#002639` | (0, 38, 57) | Backgrounds, headers, slide backgrounds |
| **Secondary** | Midnight Teal | `#003144` | (0, 49, 68) | Secondary backgrounds, sidebars, footers |
| **Accent 1** | Bright Teal | `#3ABFB9` | (58, 191, 185) | Headings, titles, hyperlinks, key highlights |
| **Accent 2** | Golden Yellow | `#FFD75E` | (255, 215, 94) | Call-to-action, emphasis, chart highlights |
| **Accent 3** | Warm Glow | `#F5D384` | (245, 211, 132) | Secondary emphasis, gradient accents |
| **Accent 4** | Ocean Teal | `#005F6F` | (0, 95, 111) | Borders, dividers, subtle accents |
| **Neutral Light** | Soft Sage | `#91BE8E` | (145, 190, 142) | Secondary charts, positive indicators |
| **Neutral Mid** | Sea Green | `#2BA19A` | (43, 161, 154) | Body accents, icons, midtone elements |
| **Text Light** | White | `#FFFFFF` | (255, 255, 255) | Body text on dark backgrounds |
| **Text Dark** | Deep Navy | `#002639` | (0, 38, 57) | Body text on light backgrounds |

---

## PowerPoint

### Slide Backgrounds
- **Title slides:** Solid `Deep Navy (#002639)` or gradient from `Deep Navy` to `Midnight Teal (#003144)`
- **Content slides:** White (`#FFFFFF`) with `Deep Navy` header bar, or light gray (`#F5F5F5`)
- **Section dividers:** Solid `Ocean Teal (#005F6F)`

### Text
- **Title text on dark backgrounds:** `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)` or `Golden Yellow (#FFD75E)`
- **Title text on light backgrounds:** `Deep Navy (#002639)`
- **Body text on dark backgrounds:** `White (#FFFFFF)`
- **Body text on light backgrounds:** `Deep Navy (#002639)`
- **Subtitle / secondary text:** `Ocean Teal (#005F6F)` or `Sea Green (#2BA19A)`

### Accent Elements
- **Shapes, icons, bullets:** `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)`
- **Emphasis / callout boxes:** `Golden Yellow (#FFD75E)` with `Deep Navy` text
- **Borders and dividers:** `Ocean Teal (#005F6F)`
- **Chart primary series:** `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)`, `Golden Yellow (#FFD75E)`, `Soft Sage (#91BE8E)`, `Sea Green (#2BA19A)`

### Suggested Font Pairing
- **Headings:** Bold sans-serif (Calibri, Montserrat, or similar)
- **Body:** Regular sans-serif (Calibri, Open Sans, or similar)

---

## Word Documents

### Page Layout
- **Header bar:** `Deep Navy (#002639)` with `White` or `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)` text
- **Footer:** `Midnight Teal (#003144)` thin line with `Ocean Teal` page numbers
- **Body background:** White

### Text Hierarchy
- **Heading 1:** `Deep Navy (#002639)`, bold, 18pt
- **Heading 2:** `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)`, bold, 14pt
- **Heading 3:** `Ocean Teal (#005F6F)`, bold, 12pt
- **Body text:** `Deep Navy (#002639)`, regular, 11pt
- **Hyperlinks:** `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)`

### Accent Elements
- **Horizontal rules:** `Ocean Teal (#005F6F)`
- **Callout / tip boxes:** `Golden Yellow (#FFD75E)` left border, light yellow (`#FFF8E1`) fill
- **Table headers:** `Deep Navy (#002639)` fill, `White` text
- **Table alternating rows:** `#F0F7F7` (tinted light teal)

## Excel

### Workbook Theme
- **Tab colors:** Rotate through `Bright Teal`, `Golden Yellow`, `Ocean Teal`, `Soft Sage`
- **Sheet background:** White

### Table Formatting
- **Header row:** `Deep Navy (#002639)` fill, `White` bold text
- **Alternating rows:** White / `#EDF5F5` (light teal tint)
- **Total row:** `Midnight Teal (#003144)` fill, `Golden Yellow (#FFD75E)` bold text
- **Borders:** `Ocean Teal (#005F6F)`, thin

### Conditional Formatting Suggestions
- **Positive / good:** `Soft Sage (#91BE8E)`
- **Warning / caution:** `Golden Yellow (#FFD75E)`
- **Negative / alert:** `#E05252` (complementary red -- not in logo, reserved for alerts)
- **Neutral / informational:** `Sea Green (#2BA19A)`

### Charts
- **Series colors (in order):** `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)`, `Golden Yellow (#FFD75E)`, `Soft Sage (#91BE8E)`, `Sea Green (#2BA19A)`, `Ocean Teal (#005F6F)`, `Warm Glow (#F5D384)`
- **Chart background:** White
- **Gridlines:** Light gray (`#E0E0E0`)
- **Axis labels:** `Deep Navy (#002639)`

---

## Email Signatures / Web

### Email Signature
- **Name:** `Deep Navy (#002639)`, bold
- **Title / tagline:** `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)`
- **Divider line:** `Golden Yellow (#FFD75E)`
- **Links:** `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)`

### Web / Markdown
- **Background:** White or `Deep Navy (#002639)` for dark mode
- **Primary headings:** `Bright Teal (#3ABFB9)`
- **Accent highlights:** `Golden Yellow (#FFD75E)`
- **Code blocks:** `Midnight Teal (#003144)` background, `White` text
